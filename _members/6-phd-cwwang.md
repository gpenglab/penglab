Hello, I am Chuwei Wang,a Ph.D. candidate in the Peng Lab, I joined the Peng Lab in 2022. I focus on the enhancers regulating the fate of neuromesodermal progenitors (NMPs). NMPs are a group of bipotential progenitors in the tail of vertebrate embryo, contributing to the development of somite and nervous system. Enhancers play an important role in the maintenance and differentiation of NMPs. Finding the critical enhancers in NMPs will shed light on the developmental biology.
